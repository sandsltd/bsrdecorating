7.3 What You Receive

After every agent run, you receive an email report containing:

  • Keyword ranking changes (which keywords moved up or down)
  • New blog post details (title, target keyword, word count)
  • Internal links added across the site
  • Competitor activity (any new content from competitors)
  • SEO recommendations for the next period
  • A summary of all work completed
Frequency: The agent runs automatically every 5 days via GitHub Actions, with a randomised timing window for natural publishing patterns. It can also be triggered manually at any time.

7.4 Content Quality Assurance

Every piece of generated content follows strict guidelines:

  • Locally authentic: References specific Exeter/Topsham streets, landmarks, and areas
  • Data-informed: Uses real Exeter pricing data and Devon market statistics
  • Heritage-aware: Conservation area rules, listed building requirements, traditional materials
  • Brand-consistent: BSR's professional-but-approachable tone, eco-conscious values
  • SEO-optimised: Target keyword in title, subheadings, and naturally throughout body text
  • Action-oriented: Every post includes a natural call-to-action for free quotes
